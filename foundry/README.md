# Foundry

The Foundry is where we walk alongside projects and founders as they design, build, and ship tools in the permissionless tech ecosystem.

We don't act like a VC, an incubator, or a boss. We act as collaborators, fellow builders, and stewards of shared infrastructure and shared values.

## Current Projects

- [bitchat](https://bitchat.free) - Peer-to-peer messaging over Bluetooth mesh
- [Cashu](https://cashu.space) - Privacy-preserving ecash on Bitcoin
- [Chorus](https://chorus.community) - Decentralized community platform for creators
- [diVine](https://divine.video) - Short-form looping video on Nostr
- [Flotilla](https://flotilla.social) - Customizable community platform on Nostr
- [Marmot](https://github.com/marmot-protocol/marmot) - End-to-end encrypted group messaging protocol
- [Shakespeare](https://shakespeare.diy) - Open-source AI builder
- [White Noise](https://whitenoise.chat) - Private messaging on Nostr with MLS
